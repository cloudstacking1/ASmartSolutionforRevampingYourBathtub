What is Tub Refinishing and Why Choose It?

Tub refinishing—also known as bathtub reglazing or tub glazing—is a cost-effective way to refurbish your existing bathtub instead of replacing it entirely. This process involves repairing any chips or cracks and applying a durable coating that restores the tub’s surface to a like-new shine.

Step-by-Step Tub Refinishing Process at NYC Tub Refinishing

  • Inspection and Assessment

    • Our team visits your home at 136 W 71st St, New York, NY 10023, to inspect your bathtub and surrounding tiles, evaluating damage and discussing your preferences.

  • Preparation and Cleaning

    • We thoroughly clean the tub surface to remove soap scum, oils, and mineral deposits, ensuring perfect adhesion of the refinishing products.

  • Repairs

    • Any chips, cracks, or rust spots are carefully repaired using professional-grade fillers and sanding for a smooth base.

  • Masking and Protection

    • Surrounding areas are masked off to protect walls, floors, and fixtures from overspray.

  • Application of Primer

    • A bonding primer is sprayed onto the tub to create a strong foundation for the finish.

  • Refinishing Coating Application

    • We apply multiple layers of high-quality epoxy or urethane coating, which cures to a hard, glossy surface.

  • Curing and Final Inspection

    • The tub cures for 24-48 hours. Afterward, we inspect for quality and detail, then clean up the workspace.

Maintenance Tips to Prolong Your Tub Refinishing

    To keep your refinished tub looking pristine:

  • Avoid abrasive cleaning pads or harsh chemicals.

  • Use mild, non-abrasive cleaners.

  • Clean spills and stains promptly.

  • Avoid dropping heavy objects in the tub.

  • Ventilate your bathroom to reduce moisture buildup.
